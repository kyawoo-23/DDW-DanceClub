Uniform Guide

Beginner Class Uniform Guide

The Beginner Class Dance Exam allows young dancers to demonstrate musicality in ballet, enhance their dance performance skills and demonstrate confidence in a dance routine.

For female ballet dancers, the correct uniform is a ruched sleeveless or short sleeve scoop neck dance leotard in blue, mulberry, lavender or navy. It should be made from cotton or lycra fabric and will need a matching elasticated belt. Girls will also need the correct pink soft ballet shoes made from satin, canvas or leather as dancing barefoot is not permitted.

For male ballet dancers the uniform is a white short sleeved leotard or well fitting white t shirt with navy shorts. They must also wear white ballet shoes in canvas or leather.

Intermediate Class Uniform Guide

The Intermediate Class Dance Exam is essential for young dancers to demonstrate their coordination of the whole body, barre exercises, classical dance and character dance, and demonstrate their posture, timing and music.

For female ballet dancers, it is essential that they wear a classic style of dance leotard in matt fabric in black, burgundy or royal blue. The skirt worn should be made from a soft, sheer material, such as a Georgette or chiffon and should be mid calf length.

For male ballet dancers, should wear a short sleeved or sleeveless white matt leotard. If the leotard is felt to be unsuitable, then a well fitting white t shirt is fine. Navy stirrup tights are a must, as are white ballet shoes in canvas or leather and short white socks.

Advanced Class Uniform Guide

For the Advanced Class Dance Exam, students should demonstrate confidence and stamina, engage the audience and demonstrate spatial awareness, control and coordination.

For female dancers, should wear a sleeveless dance leotard in black, royal blue, navy or purple.

For male dancers, should wear a white, navy or black dance leotard, black or navy tights, black or white socks and black or white shoes with elastic.
