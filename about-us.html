About Us

Lindisfarne's best Ballroom Dancing Club

Who are we?

Lindisfarne Ballroom Dancing Academy was founded by a couple in 2016 due to demand from clients. It was at first a small group of handful dance enthusiasts but now this has grown significantly and lessons and dances are being held three times a week. The club includes ballroom dance classes for beginners, intermediate and advanced dancers. They also hold a weekly dance for all members on a Saturday night. The club is located in the center of Lindisfarne Island.

Our mission is to form students, through our specialized training, at various levels of classical dance, contemporary dance, latin rhythms, yoga and zumba, in the interest of art, health and culture.
