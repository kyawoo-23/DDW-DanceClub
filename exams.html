Available Exams

Beginner Class Exam

The outcome of this curriculum is for young dancers to move with increased control and learn co-ordination and confidence, and laying the foundations for them to be able to learn and master more complex dance moves later on. This curriculum helps to develop the stamina, creativity and coordination of the students, which will stay with them throughout their dance career.


Date: Nov 2, 2022

Requirements: Students with >30hrs in beginner class

Costs: £100


Female Solo Seal Examination

Intermediate Dance Exam candidates will demonstrate confidence and stamina, engage the audience and demonstrate spatial awareness, control and coordination. Optional items are a 2cm elastic waistband which is the same colour as the leotard, a half tutu for variation 1 or skirt for variation 2. The tone of these should match the leotard.


Date: Nov 13, 2022

Requirements: Advanced Female students

Costs: £300

Date: Nov 14, 2022
